# Community / 社区交流群

## About Giien Global

Giien Global focuses on practical products, systems, and workflows for cross-border operators, AI product builders, and independent makers.

The goal is not to collect shiny concepts. The goal is to build tools that reduce friction, create leverage, and turn scattered effort into repeatable output. Jien Browser Fleet is part of that direction: a local-first operating surface for browser environments, ecommerce accounts, proxy health, and repeatable workflows.

If you are building AI products, browser automation systems, ecommerce workflows, or tools for global markets, you are welcome to join the community.

## Who This Is For

- Cross-border sellers and ecommerce operators
- AI product builders
- Indie makers
- Developers, designers, marketers, and operators exploring global products
- People building tooling, workflows, automation, and operational systems

Please keep discussions practical, respectful, and relevant to AI products, ecommerce tooling, automation workflows, and international growth.
